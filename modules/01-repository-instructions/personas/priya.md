## üîó Connections to Your Work

### Skills You're Building
- **Pattern recognition**: Seeing and understanding team conventions
- **Confident coding**: Writing code that passes review
- **Accelerated learning**: Understanding through consistent, guided examples
- **Professional development**: Growing faster with AI as a teaching assistant

### How This Helps Your Real Work

As a junior developer, your job is to learn and contribute. But traditional learning has problems:
- **Implicit patterns**: Seniors "just know" the right way
- **Trial and error**: Learn by making mistakes in reviews
- **Fear of asking**: Don't want to look incompetent
- **Slow feedback loops**: Days between code and review feedback

**Repository instructions solve all four:**

1. **Explicit patterns**: Standards are documented, not in people's heads
2. **Learn by doing correctly**: AI generates correct code from the start
3. **Safe learning**: AI answers "how should I do this?" without judgment
4. **Immediate feedback**: Get it right the first time, or iterate quickly

### Artifacts You Create
- Character quotes endpoint following team patterns
- Growing confidence in your ability to contribute
- Understanding of why patterns matter, not just what they are
