## üí≠ Design Decisions

### Why Not Organization Skills?
- Skills are typically repo-specific
- Focus on instructions (more common enterprise need)
- Spaces provide better cross-repo skill sharing

### Why Include Free-Tier Exercises?
- Code review standards don't require Business/Enterprise
- Onboarding works with any tier
- Shows enterprise benefits at all levels

### Why "Tuesday 9:00 AM"?
- Natural continuation from Module 9 (Monday 6:00 PM)
- Overnight reflection: "Now what?"
- Signals shift from building to scaling

### Exercise Ordering Logic
1. **Instructions first** - Foundation (most common need)
2. **Review standards** - Quality gates (applies to all)
3. **Spaces** - Knowledge sharing (Enterprise showcase)
4. **Metrics** - Measurement (data-driven decisions)
5. **Onboarding** - Full circle (enabling others)
